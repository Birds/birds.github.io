Common questions

Is my data stored anywhere?
No. Everything you enter stays on your device. Nothing is transmitted.

Why did my values disappear?
Values persist across sessions automatically. If they were lost, the clear button (trash icon, top right) may have been tapped.

Where can I get the app?
MedicCalc is available on the App Store.

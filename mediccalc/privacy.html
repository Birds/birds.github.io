Privacy Policy

The short version: MedicCalc doesn't collect, store, or transmit any data. Everything stays on your device.

Last updated: April 2026

Data collection

MedicCalc collects no data. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, or any other information about you or your device.

All values you enter — weight, fluid targets, infusion details — are stored locally on your device using iOS standard storage. They never leave your device.

Third parties

MedicCalc contains no third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting tools, or tracking SDKs of any kind.

No data is shared with any third party because no data is collected in the first place.

Network access

MedicCalc does not require or use a network connection. The app functions entirely offline. No outbound requests are made at any time.

Children's privacy

MedicCalc does not collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes in a future update, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date at the top.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to dev@taekang.org.
